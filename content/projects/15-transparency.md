The BetterGov.ph Transparency Dashboard is an online platform designed to provide Philippine citizens, businesses, and visitors with access to comprehensive government procurement and budget records. It enables users to search through millions of data entries related to government contracts, procurement activities, and financial allocations from 2000 to 2025.
