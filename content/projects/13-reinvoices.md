re:invoices is a modern invoice management platform designed to simplify accounts payable processes through AI-powered automation. It allows users to upload, forward, or scan invoices, with automatic data extraction and organization. The platform offers features such as secure cloud storage, dedicated email addresses for invoice forwarding, intelligent auto-tagging, bulk processing, team email management, and advanced reporting.
