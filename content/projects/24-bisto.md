Bisto Proyekto is a citizen-powered reporting platform dedicated to monitoring government infrastructure projects in the Philippines. It aims to promote transparency and accountability by providing a comprehensive database of ongoing, completed, and upcoming infrastructure projects across various regions. The platform allows users to filter projects based on year, location, category, and status, and offers visualizations through tables and maps. It is an initiative supported by multiple organizations, including TAPAT, BetterGov.ph, DPWH, CBCP, and others, emphasizing community engagement and oversight in public infrastructure development. The site encourages public participation through volunteer opportunities and provides detailed project information to foster informed citizen involvement.
